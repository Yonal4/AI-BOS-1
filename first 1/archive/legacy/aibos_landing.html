FAQ

Common questions

What happens if the AI employee makes a mistake? +
Every new customer starts in "co-pilot mode" — AI employees draft actions for your review before executing. As you build trust, you can expand autonomy. Every action has a full reasoning log and reversible undo. Low-confidence actions are automatically routed to your approval queue.
How long does setup take? +
Your first AI employee completes its first autonomous action within 30 minutes of signup. The Company Brain onboarding is guided — upload your docs, connect your CRM, define your ICP. Our templates for top industries (SaaS, e-commerce, agencies) make it even faster.
Is my company data secure? +
Yes. We never share your data with LLM providers for training. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We're working toward SOC 2 Type II certification (available on Enterprise plans). GDPR-compliant architecture from day one, with data residency options available.
What integrations do you support? +
Native integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, Slack, Notion, and LinkedIn. Growth+ plans include Zapier access (5,000+ apps). We're adding new native integrations every month. Request yours via the integrations dashboard.
Can AI employees talk to each other? +
Yes — cross-agent coordination is the core differentiator of AI BOS. When Aria closes a deal, she can automatically trigger Marcus to start onboarding and Lexi to update nurture campaigns. This coordination is available on Growth and above plans.
How is this different from Zapier or Make? +
Zapier automates rule-based workflows — IF this, THEN that. AI BOS AI employees reason, adapt, and make judgment calls. Aria doesn't just forward a lead to a template — she reads the prospect's LinkedIn, cross-references your ICP criteria, and writes a genuinely personalized email. The Company Brain gives context that no automation tool can replicate.
